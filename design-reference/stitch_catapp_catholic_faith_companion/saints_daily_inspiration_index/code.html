Saint Anthony of Padua

Feast Day: June 13

"The man who is filled with the Holy Spirit speaks in different languages. These different languages are different ways of witnessing to Christ."

Known as the "Evangelical Doctor" and the patron saint of lost things, St. Anthony was a powerful Franciscan preacher and teacher. Born in Lisbon, his life was marked by a deep devotion to the Eucharist and a miraculous ability to reach the hearts of the most hardened sinners.
