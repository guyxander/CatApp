Biography & Journey

Ordained in 2008 for the Archdiocese of Lagos, Fr. Jude Okonkwo has spent over fifteen years fostering spiritual growth in diverse urban and rural parishes. His vocational path began in the heart of Onitsha, influenced by the vibrant faith of his community.

After completing his theological studies at Saints Peter and Paul Major Seminary, Bodija, he has served as a chaplain for various youth organizations, bringing a modern and compassionate approach to traditional liturgy.
